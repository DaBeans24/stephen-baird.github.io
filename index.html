About Me

Junior at Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University (Daytona Beach), pursuing a B.S. in Aerospace Engineering on the Rocket Propulsion track (GPA ~3.3). Interests include propulsion systems, spacecraft mission design, and applying machine learning to space operations. Decathlete on ERAU Track & Field; Senior Resident Advisor and student leader focused on building strong technical and community outcomes.

Projects

  • ML-Based RSO Prediction

    Used satellite data and Python/ML (NNs + regression) to analyze orbital patterns and predict Resident Space Object motion for safer operations and conjunction avoidance.

  • 2-Stage Solid Rocket Design

    Designed a two-stage solid launch vehicle concept for ~4,750 kg to LEO. Performed performance modeling with MATLAB and CEA-based propellant properties.

  • Boston Dynamics Tail Mod (Concept)

    Robotic tail concept for leg-failure recovery on a quadruped; modeled mechanisms and control sketch with CAD and Arduino-style prototyping.

Research
